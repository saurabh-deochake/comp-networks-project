About Us

Our idea originated and evolved in Computer Network course in Rutgers University

  • February 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It started when we were pondering upon different social media and trying to find out what we could do at a particular location. We were able to combine different posts our friends posted but we weren't sure who we might find and how long we should stay?

  • March 2017

    An Agency is Born

    We decided that this is a real problem that everyone faces. We have all these tools but there is no way to merge them and find out one simple answer! We created a platform that would combine all your friends + events near you.

  • April 2017

    Transition to Full Service

    Different social media are integrated together to create a platform which gives you information about events and people you might know attending. This would help you network, and make you more likely to go outsideto a particular event. It would also give you details about what kind of event it is and how long it will last!

  • May 2017

    Coming Soon Phase...

    Algorithms would store and detect all the similarities between different places on instagram, and Facebook. Instead of only looking at hashtags, it would also look at pictures. Map all usernames from different social media to point to one specific user
